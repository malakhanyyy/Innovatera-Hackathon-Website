About the Hackathon

Empowering the Next Generation of Female Innovators

The Innovatera Hackathon is an initiative designed to inspire and empower women in Egypt through technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Organized in collaboration with UFE, health and environment entities, and tech communities across Egypt and worldwide.

Teams compete to build mobile-first, AI-enabled digital solutions rooted in local culture with real-world scalability — then pitch live before judges at Minvent UFE.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Selection Criteria

01

Women-Led or Women-Focused

Developed by women, or directly addressing key challenges faced by women in Egypt.

02

Team of 1 to 5 Members

Students or alumni from UFE or any other university. We highly encourage majority-women teams.

03

Mobile-First & Scalable

Preferably mobile-first applications that are accessible, scalable, and built for real-world use.

04

English & / or Arabic

Projects should be developed in English and/or Arabic to ensure inclusivity and local reach.

Culturally Rooted

Solutions rooted in local culture and leveraging local or community resources for maximum impact.

06

AI Prototypes Encouraged

Competition Protocol

Rules & Guidelines

Original Work & Intellectual Integrity

All software architectures, database integrations, and digital structures presented must be authored purely during the official building window. Reusing old commercial code blocks is strictly prohibited.

Team Structure & Cooperation

Cross-disciplinary groups combining design, product analysis, and software optimization fields are strongly encouraged. Cross-university collaborations are fully allowed.

Open Source & Submission Openness

To ensure transparent judging, execution repositories (such as GitHub accounts) must be completely public or explicitly shared with the technical evaluation panel.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any registration fee to participate?
No, participation in the Innovatera Hackathon is completely free for all selected teams.
Do I need a fully formed team to apply?
You can apply as a solo participant or as a team of up to 5 members. However, we highly recommend having a team with diverse skills (tech, business, design) to build a strong MVP.
Can non-UFE students apply?
Absolutely! The hackathon is open to students and alumni from all Egyptian universities, provided the project meets the women-focused or green tech criteria.
Who owns the intellectual property (IP) of the projects?
You do! Teams retain full ownership of the intellectual property they develop during the hackathon.
Do I need to have a fully working app by the final day?
You need to have a functional Minimum Viable Product (MVP) or a highly interactive prototype that demonstrates your core solution to the judges.
